About Me

Emmanuel Mugambi Atela is an aspiring leader, entrepreneur, and advocate for positive change. He is currently running for Member of County Assembly (M.C.A) of Muthara Ward, championing the slogan 'Youths for Change, Shaping a Better Future for Muthara.' With a strong commitment to empowering the youth and transforming his community,

Emmanuel's vision is focused on creating sustainable development and opportunities for all.Professionally,
Emmanuel is the CEO and founder of BlueSky Technologies, a forward-thinking company dedicated to technological innovation and solutions.
His leadership at BlueSky Technologies has driven the company to new heights, making significant strides in the tech industry under his guidance.Emmanuel is not only known for his professional achievements but also for his personal qualities.
Described as a visionary leader and compassionate individual,
Emmanuel aims to make a lasting impact in Meru County, with aspirations for a more equitable and prosperous future. His dedication to public service and technological advancement marks him as a promising figure in the region's political and economic landscape.
